    Final step

    Finally, once you have obtained an education, gained experience, obtained a certification (recommended but not necessary), created a portfolio, and determined what you would like to specialize in, you can now start applying for jobs. The job application process can be confusing and stressful for some individuals; to counter the confusion and stress, it is best to start identifying your career goals, and determining the jobs you would prefer based off your experience.
