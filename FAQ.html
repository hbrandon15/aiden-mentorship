    How long does it take to become a software engineer?

    Since obtaining a bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field is the most common route for getting an education, it takes around four years to become a software engineer. However, this can vary depending on if you attend a bootcamp or self-study; coding bootcamps and self-study are education routes that take less time than bachelor's degree.
